About EDU TrackR

EDU TrackR is a modern online study planner designed to help students manage their time, tasks, and notes efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make studying smarter, not harder. We provide a simple and organized platform to plan your day, track tasks, store notes, and stay motivated.

Key Features

  • Task Management: Keep track of your assignments and deadlines.
  • Timetable Planner: Organize your daily study schedule.
  • Notes Section: Store and review all your important notes.
  • Motivation Section: Stay inspired with daily motivational quotes.

Why Choose Us?

Whether you are preparing for exams or just want to manage your daily studies, EDU TrackR helps you stay organized and focused.
